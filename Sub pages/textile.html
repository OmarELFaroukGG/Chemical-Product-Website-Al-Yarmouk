Textile

1. Wetting Agent

The chemical which is used for quickly moisten or watering the textile materials is called wetting agent.


2. Dye fixing Agents

Dye fixing agent is nothing but a chemical that helps to fix up the dye particle into inner position of the fibre with the bondage.

3. Oxidizing and Reducing Agent

The chemical that will help to increase oxygen or electronegative part and release the hydrogen is known as oxidizing & reducing agent.
